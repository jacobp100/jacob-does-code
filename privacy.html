Privacy Policy

All data you create is stored locally on your device, and is not uploaded to any servers.

Neither usage data nor analytics are collected.

For TechniCalc, crash reports are generated by and uploaded to the third party, Sentry. Crash reports contain only the information on where in the code the crash occurred, details about the device (make and model etc.), and details about the operating system (name and version). No personal information is uploaded.
